## Objective

Stand up job-costed construction books for Raves inside Blueprint by tailoring the chart of accounts, loading opening balances, wiring accounts receivable and accounts payable, confirming the financial statements, and completing a first month-end close — so profit, receivables, and payables are current in the same system the work already lives in.

## Business Outcome

At the end of this engagement, Raves should have:

- a construction chart of accounts tailored to how Raves runs jobs,
- opening balances loaded and a trial balance that ties out,
- invoices and customer payments flowing into accounts receivable with aging,
- vendor bills and payments flowing into accounts payable with aging,
- income statement and balance sheet available on demand,
- a completed and locked first month-end close,
- a trained accounting owner and office team.

## Scope

### 1. Chart Of Accounts Tailoring

Deliverables:

- Review the built-in construction chart of accounts with Raves.
- Adjust account names, job-cost categories, and any Raves-specific accounts.
- Confirm handling of retainage receivable/payable and work-in-progress accounts.

Client dependency:

- Raves provides a short chart-of-accounts review session.
- Accounting owner: Raves to confirm by kickoff.

### 2. Opening Balances

Deliverables:

- Load opening balances from a recent trial balance or accounting export provided by Raves.
- Reconcile the loaded opening trial balance so it ties out before go-live.
- Document the as-of date for opening balances.

Client dependency:

- Raves provides opening balances: a recent trial balance, a QuickBooks/accounting export, or accountant-provided figures.
- Opening-balance source: Raves to confirm by kickoff.

### 3. Accounts Receivable

Deliverables:

- Configure customer records and invoicing.
- Confirm the path from a won quote to an invoice where Raves wants it.
- Verify payment recording and accounts-receivable aging.
- Confirm branded invoice PDF output.

### 4. Accounts Payable

Deliverables:

- Configure vendor records and bill entry.
- Verify bill payment recording and accounts-payable aging.
- Confirm branded bill PDF output.

### 5. Statements, Close, And Training

Deliverables:

- Confirm income statement and balance sheet reflect Raves activity correctly.
- Run a first month-end close in Blueprint and lock the period.
- One accounting-owner walkthrough and one office-team walkthrough.
- Closeout note with completed items, open items, and recommended next steps.

Out of scope (available as the expanded Books Complete package):

- Bank reconciliation.
- Cash-flow statement.
- Migration of historical books from current accounting software.
- A parallel month run against existing books.
- 30-day hypercare.

Also out of scope:

- Tax filing or tax advice.
- Payroll processing.
- New paid third-party tools unless approved in writing.

## Timeline

1. Kickoff + chart-of-accounts review: upon written approval, initial payment/payment step confirmation, and accounting-owner availability.
2. Opening balances loaded and tied out: target week 1-2 after opening-balance readiness.
3. A/R and A/P wired and verified: target week 2-3 after opening-balance readiness.
